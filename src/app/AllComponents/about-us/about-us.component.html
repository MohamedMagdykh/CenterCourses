Avicenna Academy is an Australian private institute aims to provide quality and deep education to Primary and Secondary students with a focus on Mathematics and Science via online tutoring and support. Our main programs is developed based on Singapore's education system which has been consistently ranked as one of the highest in the world by the Organization for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD). We do also provide customised programs for different countries' curriculums depending on the student'sw requirements. Our tutors are well selected - to ensure they can effectively value-add to the learning process - qualified, experienced, and passionate. they consider every student as a challenge, track the progress & the soft skills of the student in the class, and transparently share the feedback with the parents.

Empowering every student with our innovative teaching methodology to enhance their confidence in learning and unleash thier potential
